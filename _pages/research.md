### Publications

**ATRNet-STAR: Depression Angle Diversity, Few-Shot Recognition, and Cross-Dataset Generalization in SAR Automatic Target Recognition**  
Mousa Shaya · Shaya Software and Systems LLC \| Arizona State University  
*arXiv preprint, May 2026*

A systematic study of SAR ATR generalization across depression angles, data regimes, and datasets using the ATRNet-STAR (40 classes, 4 angles) and MSTAR benchmarks. Key findings: adding 60° training data to a 15°-only baseline improves EOC accuracy by +30.4 pp; prototypical classification achieves 98%+ accuracy with only 5 reference images per class; test-time augmentation hurts SAR ATR by −24.3 pp; DINOv2 optical features are near-chance (6.1%) on SAR.
