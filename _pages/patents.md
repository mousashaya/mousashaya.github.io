## ECG Pattern Recognition Patents

All patents awarded for work at Hewlett-Packard / Agilent Technologies, Andover MA (1980–1995). These algorithms are deployed in intensive care units worldwide.

1. **US 4,664,116** — Pace Pulse Identification Apparatus
2. **US 4,832,041** — Pace Pulse Eliminator
3. **US 4,539,999** — Method and Device for Subtracting a Pacer Signal from an ECG Signal
4. **US 4,838,278** — Paced QRS Complex Classifier
5. **US 5,447,164** — Interactive Medical Information Display System and Method for Displaying User-Definable Patient Events
6. **Classification A61B5/7264** — Classification of Physiological Signals Using Neural Networks, Statistical Classifiers, and Expert Systems

These patents represent the first system in the industry to successfully monitor patients with pacemakers in real-time clinical environments — built before modern ML frameworks existed.
